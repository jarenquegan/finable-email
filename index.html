We are FINable, a team formed through the Asian Undergraduate Symposium (AUS), organized by the National University of Singapore (NUS), dedicated to improving financial literacy among youth through inclusive and accessible educational tools. Our initiative, FINable: A Financial Literacy Card Game, is designed to teach essential money management skills to young individuals, including those from low-income communities and persons with disabilities.

FINable stands out by offering:

  • Inclusive Design – Featuring Braille and textured elements for learners with visual impairments
  • Interactive Learning – Using game-based mechanics to make financial education engaging
  • Real-World Relevance – Teaching practical concepts such as budgeting, saving, needs vs. wants, and more

We are currently seeking partner organizations to help bring FINable to communities where financial education is most needed. We believe your organization's mission aligns with our goals, and we would love to explore ways we can collaborate through the workshop we have in store for the schools and organizations, with a special introduction to our inclusive card game, FINable.
